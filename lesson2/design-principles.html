Design Principles

White Space
Microsoft Home Page

Microsoft's home page used white space to make the products and services offered on this home page pop, in a sense.

Contrast
Hypixel Skyblock Wiki

Hypixel Skyblock's Wiki is a good example of contrast because the gold and white text is easily visible against the black background.

Rule of Thirds
HubSpot Home Page

HubSpot's website is a good example of the Rule of Thirds because most of the interactive content, the tools designers want users to click on, appear on the top left of the page.

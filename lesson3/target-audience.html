Oregon City Chamber of Commerce

Target Audience

Our target audience is consumers and businesspeople alike!
Anyone with an interest in learning about the local shops in our wonderful community are welcome on this site.

Mike

Mike is a Businessman from out of town looking for vendors in Oregon City he can ask to stock his pencil sharpeners. Mike will use the Chamber of Commerce site on a desktop as he browses for worthy local vendors.

Ike

Ike recently moved his family from out of state and is looking to get a bearing of what services are near him. Ike will likely browse the Chamber of Commerce site on his phone when taking breaks unpacking.

Possible Scenarios

  • Does that store carry a polo shirt in my size? What color options exist? Price?
  • How do I make a CSS-driven drop-down navigation bar?
  • I need to transfer funds from one account to another at my bank.
  • Who won the game last night? What was the score?
  • What's the weather going to be this weekend at the trail I'm planning to hike?
